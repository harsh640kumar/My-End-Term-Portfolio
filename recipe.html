Italian Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta or Bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino or Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté pancetta/bacon until crisp. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute, then remove garlic.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and grated cheese together.
  4. Drain spaghetti and quickly toss it into the pan with pancetta, then remove from heat and stir in the egg-cheese mixture. Mix quickly to avoid scrambling the eggs.
  5. Season with black pepper and extra cheese. Serve immediately.
